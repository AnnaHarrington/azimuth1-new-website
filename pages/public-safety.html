MiniCarta

MiniCarta is a geospatial data collection and sharing platform we offer because we routinely see a need for a simple, fast, and accurate data collection and tagging system for field work. MiniCarta serves lots of uses, but we originally built it for disaster response teams. You may find it useful for tagging and sharing hazards, search locations, incident command icons, and tracking teams.

SARCAT

SARCAT is a records management system for search and rescue teams. Keeping thorough records on missions is important for tracking training hours, mission hours, reducing libility, and for data analysis through the international search and rescue database, ISRID. SARCAT is free and open source.
